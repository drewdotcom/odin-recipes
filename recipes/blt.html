BLT Recipe

This BLT recipe is to die for!

No matter how simple a BLT may be is ALWAYS hits the spot.

The following ingredients is all that you need:

  • Bread of your choice
  • Mayo
  • Tomato
  • Bacon
  • Lettuce

Follow the steps below

  1. Toast the bread
  2. Spread mayo on bread
  3. Top with Bacon.Lettuce,Tomato
  4. Slice and Serve
